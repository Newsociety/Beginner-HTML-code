Pets Fly Free*

We are proud to introduce a new benefit for all our frequent flyer members: Any time you fly with us, you can now bring along your cat, dog, bird, rabbit, ferret, hedgehog, aligator, or any other household pet free of charge.

It is easy as 1-2-3-4-5-6

  1. If you have not already done so, join our popular Frequent Flyer program
  2. Tell all your friends about our great airline
  3. Purchase a ticket to any of our destinations
  4. Pay for your ticket
  5. Purchase an approved carrying case for your pet
  6. Have your pet quarantined as per local restrictions**
  7. Bring along your pet for free

*Limit 1 pet per ticketed passenger. Pet must weigh less than 50 pounds. People & children do not qualify as a pet.

**The Islands of Hawaii have a mandatory quarantine period for incoming pets. Nui Manu Airlines can work miracles if the need arises!
